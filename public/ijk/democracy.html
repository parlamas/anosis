The Basics on Democracy

Contrary to popular belief, save 50 years in Classical Athens, democracy has never been practiced.

Also contrary to popular belief, popular voting and democracy are mutually exclusive.

To make sense of all this, we need to first define democracy.

Democracy is the exercise of direct personal power by each able adult on a rotaing basis.

This is the apogee of decentralization of power and, in practical terms, it means that each citizen serves, depending on the demographics, let us say, 2 weeks on the police force, 2 weeks as a legislator in the city council, and 2 weeks as a judge in court.

The gist of it is that you do not have to vote to delegate duties and responsibilities to others because you now exercise power yourself.

It is everyone's responsibility to run their own lives but by delegating that responsibility to others you create the opportunities for corruption of all sorts to take root.

There is a principle in politics whose merit is undoubtful:

Long term or permanent power and democracy are mutually exclusive.

Grundlæggende om Demokrati

I modsætning til populær tro, undtagen 50 år i det klassiske Athen, er demokrati aldrig blevet praktiseret.

Også i modsætning til hvad folk tror, udelukker folkeafstemning og demokrati gensidigt.

For at forstå alt dette, skal vi først definere demokrati.

Demokrati er udøvelsen af direkte personlig magt af hver dygtig voksen på rotationsbasis.

Dette er toppen af decentraliseringen af magten, og i praksis betyder det, at hver borger, afhængigt af demografien, lad os sige, 2 uger i politiet, 2 uger som lovgiver i byrådet og 2 uger. som dommer i retten.

Kernen i det er, at du ikke behøver at stemme for at uddelegere pligter og ansvar til andre, fordi du nu selv udøver magten.

Det er alles ansvar at styre deres eget liv, men ved at uddelegere dette ansvar til andre skaber du mulighederne for, at korruption af enhver art kan slå rod.

Der er et princip i politik, hvis fortjeneste er utvivlsomt:

Langsigtet eller permanent magt og politik udelukker hinanden.
