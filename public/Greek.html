My future dreams in Denmark

Mine fremtidsdrømme i Danmark

  1. My wife and I decided to move to Denmark with our three children to escape political corruption in Greece.

    Min kone og jeg besluttede at flytte til Danmark med vores tre børn for at undslippe politisk korruption i Grækenland.

  2. My wife, who is a sociologist, has been working as a cook in Denmark for the last two years.

    Min kone, der er sociolog, har arbejdet som kok i Danmark de sidste to år.

  3. My daughter studied computer science at the University of Souther Denmark, in Odense, and has been coding for the same firm for the last four years in Copenhagen.

    Min datter har læst datalogi på Syddansk Universitet i Odense og har kodet for samme firma de sidste fire år i København.

  4. My elder son is both in his fifth semester studying business and economics at the University of Aarhus, in Aarhus, and working at a warehouse in Herning.

    Min ældste søn er både i gang med sit femte semester og læser business og økonomi på Aarhus Universitet i Aarhus og arbejder på et lager i Herning.

  5. My younger son is in his third semester studying European studies at the University of Southern Denamark, in Sønderborg.

    Min yngste søn er på tredje semester og læser Europastudier på Syddansk Universitet i Sønderborg.

  6. So, one of my future dreams in Denmark is to see my children flourish and prosper both in their professional and personal lives.

    Så en af mine fremtidsdrømme i Danmark er at se mine børn blomstre og trives både i deres professionelle og personlige liv.

  7. Regarding my other dream, I have started a publishing business myself here in Denmark and I intend to both publish and sell the books I write.

    Angående min anden drøm, så har jeg selv startet en forlagsvirksomhed her i Danmark, og jeg har tænkt mig at både udgive og sælge de bøger, jeg skriver.

  8. I write books on practical political philosophy as well as books on comparative English and Danish grammar.

    Jeg skriver bøger om praktisk politisk filosofi samt bøger om sammenlignende engelsk og dansk grammatik.

004
Working in Denmark: advantages and disadvantages

At arbejde i Danmark: fordele og ulemper

  1. I honestly cannot think of any disadvantages with regard to working in Denmark, so I will talk only about the advantages.

    Jeg kan ærlig talt ikke komme i tanke om nogen ulemper i forhold til at arbejde i Danmark, så jeg vil kun tale om fordelene.

  2. I come from Greece, which is a thoroughly corrupt country, so it is not very difficult for me to see the advantages of working in Denmark.

    Jeg kommer fra Grækenland, som er et gennemkorrupt land, så det er ikke særlig svært for mig at se fordelene ved at arbejde i Danmark.

  3. The most important advantage is that employment is based on merit rather than on partisan connections.

    Den vigtigste fordel er, at ansættelsen er baseret på fortjeneste snarere end på partipolitiske forbindelser.

  4. The second advantage that comes to mind is that people in Denmark get a lot back for the taxes they pay, whereas in Greece you get absolutely nothing, and that is not an exaggeration.

    Den anden fordel, man tænker på, er, at folk i Danmark får meget tilbage for de skatter, de betaler, hvorimod man i Grækenland absolut intet får, og det er ikke en overdrivelse.

  5. To add hubris to irony, actual taxes in Greece are much higher than in Denmark.

    For at tilføje hybris til ironien er de faktiske skatter i Grækenland meget højere end i Danmark.

  6. An additional advantage that comes to mind is that people is that people in Denmark get a lot back for the taxes they pay, whereas in Greece, you get absolutely nothing, and that is not an exaggeration.

    En yderligere fordel, der kommer til at tænke på, er, at folk i Danmark får meget tilbage for den skat, de betaler, hvorimod man i Grækenland absolut intet får, og det er ikke en overdrivelse.

  7. Every year, the government invents new taxes unrelated to actual income, such as increase in gasoline taxes, increases in property taxes, paying highway tolls for highways which are supposed to be built in the future, as well as many other malicious tricks that government official can think of.

    Hvert år opfinder regeringen nye skatter, der ikke er relateret til den faktiske indkomst, såsom stigninger i benzinskatter, stigninger i ejendomsskatter, betaling af motorvejsafgifter for motorveje, der formodes at blive bygget i fremtiden, samt mange andre ondsindede tricks, som embedsmænd kan tænke på.

  8. The third advantage is that employers in Denmark are keen to squeeze life out of their employees. Managers are narcissistic and authoritarian, which results a lot of times in outright humiliation.

    Den tredje fordel er, at arbejdsgivere i Danmark er opsat på at presse livet ud af deres medarbejdere. Ledere er narcissistiske og autoritære, hvilket mange gange resulterer i direkte ydmygelse.

  9. A fourth advantage is that in Denmark the wages and salary are not enough for those employed to make ends meet.

    En fjerde fordel er, at lønnen i Danmark ikke rækker til, at de ansatte kan klare sig.

  10. As a rule, in Greece, even though you have a full-time job, you will need to get a secong job to be able to pay for the most basic needs of a household.

    Som regel skal du i Grækenland, selvom du har et fuldtidsjob, få et andet job for at kunne betale for en husstands mest basale behov.

  11. Another advantage for those who work in Denmark is that when the time comes, they get a decent pension, whereas in Greece, the pension definitely forces households to live in poverty.

    En anden fordel for dem, der arbejder i Danmark, er, at når tiden kommer, får de en ordentlig pension, hvorimod pensionen i Grækenland absolut tvinger husstandene til at leve i fattigdom.

  12. I could write a book about the advantages of working in Denmark versus of working in Greece, but to conlude, in Greece one has to pay from twenty to forty percent of their wages to health insurance, which in effect additional taxation. If you don't, you have to find money to pay for hospital bills or you lose your health permanently.

    Jeg kunne skrive en bog om fordelene ved at arbejde i Danmark versus at arbejde i Grækenland, men for at konkludere, så skal man i Grækenland betale fra tyve til fyrre procent af deres løn til sygesikring, hvilket i realiteten er ekstrabeskatning. Hvis du ikke gør det, skal du finde penge til at betale hospitalsregninger, ellers mister du dit helbred permanent.

  13. It is simple. The root of all evil lies in ignorance, which, in turn, leads to political corruption.

    Det er enkelt. Roden til alt ondt ligger i uvidenhed, hvilket igen fører til politisk korruption.

005
My life in Denmark: advantages and disadvantages

Mit liv i Danmark: fordele og ulemper

  1. I've been living in Denmark for a little over two years and I find every day life truly positive and rewarding.

    Jeg har boet i Danmark i lidt over to år, og jeg synes, at hverdagen er virkelig positiv og givende.

  2. I am going to start from the disadvantages only because, as far as I am concerned, there is just one: food.

    Jeg vil kun tage udgangspunkt i ulemperne, fordi der efter min opfattelse kun er én: mad.

  3. I know from personal experience that the quality of life in Denmark compared to Greece, which is where I come from, is beyond comparison on the side of Denmark.

    Jeg ved af egen erfaring, at livskvaliteten i Danmark sammenlignet med Grækenland, hvor jeg kommer fra, er uden sammenligning på Danmarks side.

  4. So, I was surprised when I realized that that is not true when it comes to food, both tastewise and qulatitywise.

    Så jeg blev overrasket, da jeg indså, at det ikke er sandt, når det kommer til mad, både smagsmæssigt og kvalitetsmæssigt.

  5. In case you didn't know, Greece is a thoroughly corrupt country, wheras in Denmark, political corruption is minimal.

    Hvis du ikke vidste det, er Grækenland et gennemkorrupt land, mens den politiske korruption i Danmark er minimal.

  6. However, it seems to me that the food authorities in Denmark have not been doing a good job.

    Dog forekommer det mig, at fødevaremyndighederne i Danmark ikke har gjort et godt stykke arbejde.

  7. Fruit and vegetables resemble are "plastic" and the only resemblance that they have to fruit and vegetables is only visual.

    Frugt og grøntsager ligner er "plastik", og den eneste lighed de har med frugt og grønt er kun visuel.

  8. The sad thing is that Danes do not have a clue because they have no means to compare, so they consume really unhealthy pesticide-laden fruit and vegetables.

    Det triste er, at danskerne ikke har en anelse, fordi de ikke har nogen midler til at sammenligne, så de indtager virkelig usund pesticidfyldt frugt og grønt.

  9. Meats, especially ground meat, taste like medicine to me and fish is practically nonexistent and when you find it comes from fish farms.

    Kød, især hakket kød, smager som medicin for mig, og fisk er praktisk talt ikke-eksisterende, og når du opdager det, kommer det fra dambrug.

  10. Thank God we have an excellent cuisine and cooking tradition in Greece. Food tastes much beeter than it does in Denmark, but nowhere near what it used to taste like thirty years ago., plus the prices are much more reasonable, even when one compares them on the basis of buying power and not income per se.

    Gudskelov har vi en fremragende køkken- og madlavningstradition i Grækenland. Mad smager meget bedre end i Danmark, men ikke i nærheden af, hvad den smagte som for 30 år siden, plus at priserne er meget mere fornuftige, selv når man sammenligner dem ud fra købekraft og ikke indkomst i sig selv.

  11. Now, let us turn to the advantages, which are numerous.

    Lad os nu vende os til fordelene, som er talrige.

  12. Personally, I enjoy the weather in Denmark because I've grown tired of sunshine and hot weather.

    Personligt nyder jeg vejret i Danmark, fordi jeg er blevet træt af solskin og varmt vejr.

  13. Next, Danes are much more polite and discreet than Greeks. They respect one another and do not constantly judge and gossip about people behind their backs.

    Dernæst er danskerne meget mere høflige og diskrete end grækerne. De respekterer hinanden og dømmer og sladrer ikke konstant om folk bag deres ryg.

  14. It is evident that in general the authorities and the people are on the same page and not in enemy camps as is the case in Greece.

    Det er tydeligt, at myndighederne og befolkningen generelt er på samme side og ikke i fjendens lejre, som det er tilfældet i Grækenland.
