What is terrorism?

Terrorism is acts of violence such as bombings, murder, rape, arson, poisoning, deprivation of water and power which are carried out by one or more armed individuals, uniformed or not, against unarmed people such as civilians, soldiers, hostages, and prisoners of war with intent to spread fear to gain political, religious, or economic advantage.
