Bolimangel i byerne

Byerne tiltrækker alle aldersgrupper, og det bliver mere og mere vanskeligt for blandt andet studerende at finde en bolig i de store byer, fordi boligpriserne er så høje. Der er samtidig mange regler for nyt boligbyggeri, så det er dyrt for byggefirmærne at bygge billige boliger.

Housing shortage in the cities

The cities attract all age groups, and it is becoming more and more difficult for students, among others, to find a home in the big cities because housing prices are so high. At the same time, there are many rules for new housing construction, so it is expensive for the construction companies to build cheap housing.
