When we had finished our prayers and viewed the spectacle, we turned in the direction of the city. At that instant, Polemarchus, the son of Cephalus, happened to catch sight of us from a distance as we were starting on our way home, and told his servant to run and ask us to wait for him. The servant took hold of me by the cloak behind, and said: Polemarchus wants you to wait.
